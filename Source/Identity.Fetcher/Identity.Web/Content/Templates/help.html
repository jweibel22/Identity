This short manual will explain all the features in the prototype.

To sum it up, this is a news aggegator site with a social twist.
Our ambition is to deliver more personalized and relevant news to you than is possible with other news aggegators.
Channel
News articles (hereafter called posts) are coming into the system via channels.
You can see your current channels on the left. You can create new channels on the profile page (see below)
Content are coming into a channel by users posting content by clicking or by re-publishing content from other channels by clicking on a post or by an RSS feeder that has been attached to the channel.
The set of users that have access to post content into the channel are called collaborators. A user becomes collaborator by having created the channel or having been granted access by another collaborator.
A channel can be public/private. A private channel can only be seen by collaborators.

Profile
Clicking on your name in the top right corner brings you to your profile page.
On the profile page you can create/delete your personal channels.
